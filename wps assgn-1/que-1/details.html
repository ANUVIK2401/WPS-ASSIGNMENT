POSTER PRESENTATION DETAILS

Students should form a team of 2 to present the poster. The poster should be cut-throat and deep with easy visual understanding. The topics of posters selected must be related to Computer Science only. The abstract of the posters must be submitted on 3rd October, 2020.

The proceedings of the conference will be published in Smart Innovation Systems and Technologies Series, Springer (Approved). Extended versions of selected best papers will be expected to appear in SCI/Scopus indexed international journals.


Conference Focus Areas

  • Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things
  • Artificial Nueral Networks
  • Computational Intelligence
  • Data Science

Poster submission guidelines


Submission of poster must be original and should not have been previously published or under consideration for publication. At least one of the authors of each accepted paper must register for the conference. All submissions are to be done electronically through easychair link.
